Adderall / Japan

20mg tablets · 30-day supply Answered in 1.8s Verified Apr 11, 2026
Banned — do not carry.
Amphetamine-based stimulants are prohibited under Japan's Stimulants Control Act. There is no tourist permit available. Carrying any quantity is a criminal offense.

What to do instead

  1. Speak to your prescriber before Apr 25.
    Discuss a short-term switch to a non-stimulant (e.g. atomoxetine) for the 14-day trip. Atomoxetine is legal in Japan with prescription.
  2. File a Yakkan Shoumei certificate for the replacement.
    Required for any prescription import. Processing takes 4–14 business days at the Kanto-Shinetsu Regional Bureau.
  3. Carry a doctor's letter translated into Japanese.
    Include diagnosis, dosage, and the reason the replacement was prescribed. Not optional at customs.
  4. Plan refills through a Japanese clinic if staying past 30 days.
    Atomoxetine is prescribed by licensed psychiatrists in most major cities. We list 9 English-speaking clinics in Tokyo.
